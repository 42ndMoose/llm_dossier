Ownership is only one layer. The stronger explanatory layer is the **censorship and compliance network** built out of regulators, NGO hubs, academic hubs, advertiser standards, public broadcasters, and platform policy.

4.1 Trusted News Initiative

The Trusted News Initiative is one of the cleanest coordination nodes because it was publicly described as a cross-organization partnership targeting election and later vaccine disinformation, and the U.S. Department of Justice later warned in antitrust litigation that coordinated viewpoint suppression can raise competition issues in the marketplace of ideas. This is also where expertise gets weaponized at scale. The same institutions that present themselves as neutral arbiters of truth also help define the taxonomies of harm, misinformation, hate, and legitimacy that downstream platforms and advertisers then enforce.

4.5 What this means for readers

The important correction is this. The network does not need to ban every dissenter. It only has to make dissent:
- harder to monetize,
- easier to throttle,
- more expensive to defend,
- less credible to casual readers because every "trusted" node keeps repeating the same verdict.

That is why this dossier treats legacy media as part of the machine, not as a neutral reporter standing outside it.
